About Us

Accra's Most
Trusted Shop

Godfred Super Shop was founded in 2018 with a simple mission: bring quality products to every Ghanaian at fair prices. Located at Mamprobi Rainbow in Accra, we've grown from a small sports equipment store to a full-service shop offering electronics, smartphones, bicycles, shoes, jerseys, houses, land and more.

We work directly with trusted suppliers and manufacturers to ensure every product you buy from us is 100% genuine and value for money. Our team is passionate about customer satisfaction — we're not happy until you are.

  • Over 700 quality products across 9 categories
  • Serving 5,000+ happy customers since 2018
  • Secure mobile money & card payments accepted
  • Fast, reliable delivery across Greater Accra
  • 7-day return and exchange policy
  • Houses and land listings for sale in Accra
  • Friendly, knowledgeable staff always ready to help
